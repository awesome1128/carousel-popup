Bacillus Subtilis

Rhizobacteria

Stimulates new growth. Increased nitrogen uptake. Production of endophytes to combat (“root rot”) pathogens that affect plant mortality. Enables more pathways between root nodules for accelerated colonization by beneficial mycorrhizae. Induction of a systemic agent in plant roots to fight disease. Increased resistance to temperature swings and other climate stresses.

Gigasporaceae

Arbuscular Mycorrhizal Fungi

Increase in plant height, circumference and root weight. Stimulates faster node growth, with larger and longer-lasting flowers. Reduces infection of roots for certain pathogens. Higher resistance to biotic stress. Increase in overall growth rate, especially for succulent and cactus plants.

SPECIFIC SINGULAR STRAINS

Gigaspora albida 1000 IP / mL

Gigaspora margarita 1000 IP / mL

Glomeraceae

Arbuscular Mycorrhizal Fungi

Improved soil aggregation due to spore hyphae. This glomus network of hyphae regulates and increases plants uptake of important nutrients like phosphorus, nitrogen and micronutrients. Assist with soil detoxification and metabolic pathways.

SPECIFIC SINGULAR STRAINS

Glomus intraradices 1000 IP / mL

Glomus fasciculatum 1000 IP / mL

Glomus mosseae 1000 IP / mL

Glomus macrocarpum 500 IP / mL

Glomus aurantium 1000 IP / mL

Scutellospora

Arbuscular Mycorrhizal Fungi

Improved phosphorus uptake. Increased phosphorus content of root shoots. Induces systemic resistance against soil pathogens and adverse climate conditions.

SPECIFIC SINGULAR STRAINS

Scutellospora nigra 500 IP / mL

Scutellospora reticulate 500 IP / mL

Scutellospora aurigloba 1000 IP / mL

Acaulospora

Arbuscular Mycorrhizal Fungi

Improves lateral root growth. Increases root length, volume and weight. Increases root-shoot ratio by up to 30%. Provides increased root mass for colonization by other beneficial mycorrhizal strains.

SPECIFIC SINGULAR STRAINS

Acaulospora foevita 500 IP / mL

Acaulospora sp. 1 500 IP / mL

Acaulospora mellea 500 IP / mL

Claroideoglomus

Arbuscular Mycorrhizal Fungi

Positive effects on root acid phosphatase activity (RAPA). P-limitation stress avoidance. Reduces fluctuations in handling of phosphate uptake. Increased plant chlorophyll content.

SPECIFIC SINGULAR STRAINS

Claroideoglomus sp.1 500 IP / mL
